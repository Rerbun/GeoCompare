Low cam

The camera is mounted lower on the car (for privacy reasons) which makes the footage seem lower than usual. The blur of the car is therefore also bigger (not to be confused with gen 2; it is not circular and there is no blur at the top of the image). Taiwan often has a wide blur as well, but the camera is not mounted as low.

Countries: Bangladesh, India, Japan, Sri Lanka, Switzerland
