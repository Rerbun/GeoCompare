While you can find unoffical street view coverage or at least photosphers from every single country in the world not every single country has offical coverage.
Countries with offical coverage are the following:



The countries marked in red are countries you will see all the time in Geoguessr and it is a safe bet to guess in one of them.
The countries marked in pink are quite rare and mostly trekker locations. I would suggest not guessing them unless there is a good hint it might be one of those countries.
The countries marked in blue you will almost never see unless you are playing a specific map that includes them. Don't guess these countries unless there is a good hint it might be one of those countries.
The countries marked in gray don't have official coverage and they should not be guessed unless the map includes unofficial coverage.
